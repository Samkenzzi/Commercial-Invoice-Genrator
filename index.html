To connect a Google Sheet:
  1. Open your Google Sheet.
  2. Go to File → Share → Publish to web.
  3. Select Entire Document and click Publish.
  4. Copy the published link. It will look something like:
    https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/your-sheet-id/pubhtml
  5. Paste the link in the prompt that appears when loading this page, or update the script to insert it manually.
